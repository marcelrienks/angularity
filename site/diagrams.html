Recommended Bolt Positions

Visual diagrams showing the recommended eccentric bolt positions for achieving target alignment values. Each wheel shows camber and caster (front) or camber and toe (rear) bolt positions.

Load data from the report page to see recommendations. The diagrams will show recommended bolt positions for each wheel based on the loaded data.

Front and rear recommendations are shown for each loaded wheel. All bolts are displayed, with placeholders for data not yet available.
